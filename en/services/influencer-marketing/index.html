What We Deliver

Our influencer marketing service is end-to-end. We handle strategy, creator sourcing, negotiation, contracting, execution, and reporting. Brands use us when they want consistent outcomes — and creators use us when they want structured partnerships that protect their image and value.

  • Creator shortlist: a curated list based on fit, credibility, and performance potential (not follower count alone).
  • Campaign brief + messaging: value proposition, angles, do’s/don’ts, and clear CTAs.
  • Negotiation + contracts: scope, timeline, deliverables, and usage rights organized and documented.
  • Approval workflow: brand-safe execution without slowing creators down.

Platforms, Formats and Use Cases

We choose platforms and formats based on the objective and the market. What wins on TikTok is not always what wins on Instagram — and what converts in Saudi Arabia may differ from Egypt depending on category and audience behavior.

  • Platforms: TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Snapchat, Facebook
  • Formats: short-form video, story sequences, creator reviews, live sessions, UGC-style ads

  • Categories: e-commerce, clinics/beauty, real estate, hospitality, fashion, apps, education

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work in Dubai, UAE, Saudi Arabia and Egypt?

Yes. We run influencer marketing across the GCC (including Dubai/UAE and Saudi Arabia) and Egypt. Strategy, creator selection and messaging are adjusted by market because buyer behavior differs by category and region.

How do you choose influencers for measurable performance?

We prioritize audience fit, credibility, content quality and engagement signals that correlate with trust. We also consider platform behavior (TikTok vs Instagram) and align the creator style with the campaign objective.

Do you handle negotiations, contracts and usage rights?

Yes. We manage negotiation and contracting, including deliverables, timelines, revisions, whitelisting/usage rights (if needed), and approval workflows — keeping execution clean and predictable.

How do you track ROI for influencer marketing campaigns?

We use a tracking layer that can include UTM links, discount codes, landing pages and platform analytics. Reporting is tied to your KPI (leads, purchases, qualified traffic, retention signals) and includes recommendations to improve the next cycle.

Can you run an always-on influencer program instead of one-off posts?

Yes. Always-on programs are ideal for brands that want predictable growth. We test creators, scale winners, and build repeatable monthly rhythms that improve performance and reduce cost volatility over time.

Do you provide scripts and content direction?

Yes. We provide messaging, angles and guidelines for creators. For higher-stakes campaigns, our Copywriting service can produce scripts, hooks and ad-ready versions to improve conversion.
