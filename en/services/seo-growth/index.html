  • Intent map: keyword clusters mapped to pages (service/category/product), not random blog posts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Should I hire an SEO agency or run Google Ads instead?

Ads can produce results faster, while SEO compounds over time. Many GCC & Egypt brands use both: SEO builds sustainable demand capture, and ads scale short-term. We focus on making SEO measurable and conversion-driven.

Can you target searches in Dubai, Saudi Arabia and Egypt specifically?

Yes. We structure pages and intent clusters around market and service/product demand. For multi-market sites, we align page architecture, internal linking and localization signals to avoid cannibalization.

Do you write content, or do we have to provide writers?

We can provide the content structure and create/upgrade pages as needed (service pages, category pages, landing pages and supporting content). If you have an internal team, we can direct them with outlines and quality standards.

What’s included in “CRO” for SEO traffic?

CRO includes page structure, CTA placement, trust signals, speed/friction fixes, form optimization, and improving offer clarity. The goal is converting search visitors — not just improving engagement metrics.

What makes e-commerce SEO different from lead generation SEO?

E-commerce SEO focuses on category/collection architecture, product discovery, and conversion on product pages. Lead generation SEO focuses on service intent pages, landing pages, and contact flows (calls/forms/WhatsApp). We build the system based on your business model.

How do you prevent “ranking but not converting”?

By aligning search intent to page intent. If a query indicates comparison, we use a comparison structure. If it indicates buying, we route to money pages with strong CTAs, proof and low-friction actions — then we measure and iterate.
